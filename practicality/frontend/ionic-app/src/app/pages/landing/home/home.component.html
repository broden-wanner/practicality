practicality

Designed for the classical musician with organization in mind.

Practicality is a yapa (yet another practice app) but for the short- and long-term. It encourages you to practice everyday and keep notes on your progress. You can keep track of your projects in a sensible and stepwise manner while also keeping up with your daily practice habits.

Notes are the main place for you to track your progress. You can write about any of what you practiced, see your project progress, and track how often you do your daily habits.

Projects are where you can outline the steps needed to master a piece or technique. Add steps, set due dates, and add reminders.
